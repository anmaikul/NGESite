Young Global Leader Programs

Young Global Leaders are committed to helping students discover their inner strengths. Success in the 21st century requires a solid sense of self-efficacy. Participants immerse themselves in interactive activities which cultivate and harness essential skills to have in the 21st century.

  • Learn to initiate and maintain meaningful conversation.
  • Assess their individual body language as well as the body language of others.
  • Evaluate and understand hidden messages projected through micro-expressions.
  • Develop winning resumes that facilitate future opportunities.
  • Participate in the Art of Small Talk workshops and master communication skills.
  • Identify personality strengths.
  • Minimize conflict and communicate effectively with different type of thinkers
  • Practice interview skills including how best conduct yourself during various types of formats typically applied by numerous internship programs, college admission officers (undergraduate and professional), and employers such as Google, Boeing, and Goldman Sachs.
  • Participate in international business etiquette workshops.
