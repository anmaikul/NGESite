Who are our Ambassadors?

The Global Ambassador program is built on the foundation previously established by participating in previous NGE workshops and programs (i.e. Global Trailblazers, Global leaders, Global Scholars, and Global Innovators). Global Ambassadors are the pinnacle of the New Global Ed community. These formidable students take their passion to the next level by furthering their research, publishing, andpresenting their findings at symposiums and global conferences.
