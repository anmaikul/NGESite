Research. Original and Independent.

Global Scholars are challenged to complete original and independent research that is characteristic ofcollege-level intellect. Students generate research inquiry, conduct a literature review, communicatewith content-area experts, develop hypotheses, initiate statistically-sound survey instruments, collect and analyze quantitative and qualitative data, as well as acquire a basic background on research methods. Each student produces a unique e-portfolio reflective of their individual passions and demonstrated interests. New Global Ed provides expertise and guidance to students, shows them how to leverage their portfolios, and maximize opportunities.

Global Research Symposium Invitation
