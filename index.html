I build skills and confidence in students, and get them into top tier schools

A sobering fact:

Elite universities are harder to get into

Not only that,

Parents, counselors, and after-school academies often provide students misleading advice using out-of-date strategies

Did you know...


  • Good test scores and GPA are not enough to get you into an elite university
  • Attending a magnet school (e.g. Whitney) doesn't give as much advantage as you think
  • Most after-school academies focus on just one aspect of the admissions process like the SAT. At worst, they are little more than a daycare service.

    • To be competitive, students need to demonstrate that they can understand and play an important role in a

    21st century, knowledge-based, global community

    How?

    Develop what I call self-efficacy:

    An individual's belief about their ability to distinguish themselves and their ideas, create and seize opportunities around them, produce useful results.

    Hear from my students

    However, this is not a short process. There are no quick solutions or easy formulas. In fact, it takes years, or the entire adolescent period, to develop this.

    You need a comprehensive, long term strategy

    I am here to fill that need.

    Introducing New Global Ed

    A rigorous and immersive educational and extracurricular experience

    Our methodology empowers students to identify, develop, and utliize their strengths and interests to become self-efficient. Student become lead architects of their own signature projects. Upon successful completion, students gain the right skills and confidence to position themselves as compelling university candidates.

    Our program includes:

  • Personal, profession growth and development training
  • An intensive research program to turn their ideas and passions into transformative Signature Projects, with an emphasis on critical thinking, and communication skills
  • Full step by step guidance through the college application process (including college essay coaching, how to approach people to write you awesome recommendations)
  • and of course, academic guidance!
