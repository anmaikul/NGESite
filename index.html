Self-Efficacy and Student Empowerment

“College admission and self-efficacy is about more than just test scores and grades. Increased global competition, concentration of wealth, misdirected fiscal policy, and antiquated pedagogy dictates that educational institutions apply a method that empowers students to identify and utilize their strengths and interests to further develop their ability to leave an imprint on the knowledge-based, global community in which we reside.”
